About Language Library

Welcome to the Language Library! This project intends to collect and organize resources for language learning and learning about language.

Through my own journey to try to teach myself languages or find ways to learn, I have found that there are few centralized places to look for resources that aren't on websites like Reddit, where posts might get deleted or that have poor search functionality. Because of this, I wanted to create a place where people interested in learning languages can find good starting places without having to spend lots of time digging through search results themnselves.

I feel it goes without saying, but I should be clear that it's just one person maintaining this site, so there may be broken links, outdated resources, or missing resources. If you find a broken link, a bug, or know of any good resources I don't have listed, please let me know!

I am starting this project with just a few languages to get a feel for building up the resources pages, and will try to continue to add new resources and languages whenever I have time!

Happy learning!
